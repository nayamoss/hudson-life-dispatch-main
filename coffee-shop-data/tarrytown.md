# Top 2 Indie Coffee Shops in Tarrytown

*Data collected: December 28, 2025*
*Based on Google reviews and ratings from 2024-2025*

## 1. Coffee Labs Roasters

**Rating:** 4.6⭐ on Google

**Address:** 7 Main Street, Tarrytown, NY 10591

**Phone:** (914) 332-1479

**Website:** [coffeelabs.com](https://coffeelabs.com)

**Specialty:** On-site coffee roasting and sustainability

**Why It's Great:** Coffee Labs Roasters stands out in Tarrytown—and throughout Westchester—for its commitment to sustainability and the art of coffee roasting. With on-site roasting, you're guaranteed some of the freshest coffee in the region.

**The Experience:**
- Watch the roasting process and learn about different coffee origins
- Smell the incredible aroma of freshly roasted beans
- Taste the difference that fresh-roasted coffee makes

**What Makes It Special:**
- **Sustainability First:** Ethically sourced beans and environmentally conscious practices
- **Education Focus:** Knowledgeable staff eager to teach about coffee
- **Fresh Roasting:** Beans roasted on-site for maximum flavor and freshness
- **Vibrant Flavors:** The freshness creates bright, complex flavor profiles

**Perfect For:**
- Coffee connoisseurs who appreciate the craft
- Anyone interested in learning about coffee origins and roasting
- Environmentally conscious consumers

**Photo Sources:** Google Maps, coffeelabs.com, Instagram @coffeelabsroasters

## 2. Muddy Water Coffee & Café

**Rating:** 4.7⭐ on Google

**Address:** 52 Main Street, Tarrytown, NY 10591

**Phone:** (914) 909-2826

**Website:** [muddywatercoffeehouse.com](https://www.muddywatercoffeehouse.com)

**Award:** Winner of "Best Cappuccino" from Westchester Magazine (2021)

**Why It's Great:** Muddy Water has earned its reputation as one of Tarrytown's premier coffee destinations. Their award-winning cappuccino is just the beginning—the entire menu reflects a commitment to coffee excellence.

**The Vibe:** Relaxed and welcoming with a comfortable atmosphere that works equally well for catching up with friends or getting work done. The space invites you to slow down and enjoy your coffee.

**What Makes It Special:**
- **Award-Winning Coffee:** Recognized by Westchester Magazine for their cappuccino
- **Relaxed Atmosphere:** A great place to work or meet friends
- **Consistency:** Regulars praise the consistently excellent quality

**Perfect For:**
- Cappuccino lovers (obviously!)
- Remote workers seeking a comfortable workspace
- Anyone looking for a reliable, quality coffee spot

**Photo Sources:** Google Maps, muddywatercoffeehouse.com, Instagram @muddywatercoffee

## About Tarrytown's Coffee Scene

Tarrytown punches above its weight with two exceptional indie coffee shops. Coffee Labs brings the art and science of roasting, while Muddy Water delivers award-winning espresso drinks in a relaxed setting. Together they represent the best of what indie coffee culture offers. Information based on recent reviews and local reputation as of December 2025.
