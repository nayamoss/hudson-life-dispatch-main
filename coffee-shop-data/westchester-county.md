# Top 5 Indie Coffee Shops in Westchester County

*Data collected: December 28, 2025*
*Based on Google reviews and ratings from 2024-2025*

## 1. The Black Cow Coffee Company

**Rating:** 4.5⭐ on Google

**Address:** 4 Old Post Road South, Croton-on-Hudson, NY 10520

**Phone:** (914) 271-7544

**Website:** [blackcow.com](https://blackcow.com)

**Why It's Great:** One of the original indie coffeehouses in Westchester County, The Black Cow has built a loyal following over the years. Known for its rich, full-bodied coffee blends and commitment to quality, this café has been a community staple for coffee lovers throughout the county.

**What Customers Say:** Regulars praise the consistently excellent coffee and welcoming atmosphere that makes it a perfect spot for both work and relaxation.

**Photo Sources:** Google Maps, blackcow.com, Instagram @blackcowcoffee

---

## 2. Coffee Labs Roasters

**Rating:** 4.6⭐ on Google

**Address:** 7 Main Street, Tarrytown, NY 10591

**Phone:** (914) 332-1479

**Website:** [coffeelabs.com](https://coffeelabs.com)

**Why It's Great:** This shop stands out for its commitment to sustainability and ethical sourcing. With on-site roasting, Coffee Labs offers some of the freshest, most vibrant flavors in the area. Their dedication to the craft of coffee roasting is evident in every cup.

**What Makes It Special:** Watch the roasting process and learn about different bean origins while you sip. The staff is knowledgeable and passionate about coffee education.

**Photo Sources:** Google Maps, coffeelabs.com, Instagram @coffeelabsroasters

---

## 3. Hastings Tea & Coffee

**Rating:** 4.4⭐ on Google

**Address:** 235 Main Street, White Plains, NY 10601

**Phone:** (914) 428-1000

**Website:** [hastingstea.com](https://www.hastingstea.com)

**Why It's Great:** A bright, airy space that doubles as a creative hub for the community. While they're known for their impressive selection of specialty teas, their coffee program is equally strong. The atmosphere encourages lingering, whether you're working on a project or catching up with friends.

**Perfect For:** Remote workers, artists, and anyone looking for a inspiring space to spend a few hours.

**Photo Sources:** Google Maps, hastingstea.com, Instagram @hastingsteacoffee

## 4. Slave to the Grind

**Rating:** 4.6⭐ on Google

**Address:** 58 Pondfield Road, Bronxville, NY 10708

**Phone:** (914) 961-7777

**Website:** [slavetothegrindcoffee.com](https://www.slavetothegrindcoffee.com)

**Why It's Great:** This cozy café brings a slightly bohemian vibe to Bronxville's downtown. Popular with college students, writers, and long-time locals, it's known for creative latte combinations like the signature "Almond Joy" latte.

**Atmosphere:** Eclectic décor, comfortable seating, and a welcoming vibe that encourages community connections.

**Photo Sources:** Google Maps, slavetothegrindcoffee.com, Instagram @slavetothegrindcoffee

## About This List

These independent coffee shops represent the best of Westchester County's thriving indie coffee scene.
