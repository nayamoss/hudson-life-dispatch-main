## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Hudson Life Dispatch is a **free weekly events newsletter** serving Westchester County communities along the Hudson River. We automatically curate concerts, markets, workshops, and community gatherings within a 15-mile radius of Ossining, making it easy for residents to discover what's happening each week.

**Business Model**: Free newsletter supported by event venue/promoter sponsorships  
**Distribution**: Weekly email (Fridays) + web archive  
**Current Status**: Automated and working (Modal scraper + email generation)
