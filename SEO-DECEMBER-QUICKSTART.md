## TROUBLESHOOTING

**If by January 15 you have:**

**No pages indexed**:
- Resubmit sitemap in GSC
- Check robots.txt isn't blocking
- Manually request indexing for each page
- Ensure no technical SEO errors

**No keywords ranking**:
- Normal - can take 4-6 weeks
- Keep building backlinks
- Ensure content quality is high
- Check competitors aren't dominating

**No backlinks**:
- More aggressive outreach needed
- Try different email templates
- Offer more value in partnerships
- Consider guest posting

**No organic traffic**:
- Normal for first month
- Focus on rankings first, traffic follows
- Keep executing the plan
- Be patient - SEO takes time
