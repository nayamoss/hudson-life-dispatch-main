## ‚úÖ THE NEW STRATEGY: BUILD A LIFESTYLE BRAND

### Phase 1: Newsletter (Months 1-3)

- **New name/tagline**: "The Weekend Society - The best of Westchester, delivered every Tuesday"

  - "Your Ultimate Tarrytown Weekend Guide"
  - "The Hidden Gems of Westchester"

- **10% Quick Hits**: News, updates, community notices

**Growth Goal**: 1,000 subscribers by Month 3

## üìß NEWSLETTER FORMAT (THE CATSKILL CREW WAY)

**Subject**: "Your Westchester Weekend: [Themed Title]"

**Structure**:
1. **Featured Story** (60%, 1,200-1,800 words)
   - Personal narrative
   - Cultural context
   - Deep dives on places/people
   - Why it matters
   - 14-16 minute read

2. **This Weekend's Top Picks** (30%)
   - Curated list from featured story
   - Where to eat, what to see, what's happening
   - Brief (2-3 sentences each)

3. **Quick Hits** (10%)
   - News, updates, community notices
   - Weekend weather

4. **Call-to-Action**
   - Join the Weekend Society (club)
   - Shop the Weekend Society (merch)
   - Partner with us
   - Submit event

**Tone**: Personal, conversational, cultural (not corporate)  
**Length**: 2,000-3,000 words  
**Delivery**: Every Tuesday morning
