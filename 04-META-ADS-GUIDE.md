## ðŸ“Š SUCCESS METRICS

### Week 1 Goals:
- **Cost per click**: $0.50-1.50 (anything under $2 is okay)
- **Landing page conversion**: 30-50% (clicks â†’ signups)
- **Cost per subscriber**: $1-2 (optimize to $0.30-0.50)
- **Total spend**: $70-140 ($10-20/day)
- **New subscribers**: 70-140

### If Numbers Are Bad:

**High cost per click (>$2)**:
- Targeting too narrow
- Creative not compelling
- Expand audience

**Low conversion rate (<20%)**:
- Landing page problem (not ad problem)
- Fix landing page first
- Make CTA clearer

**Cost per subscriber >$2**:
- Keep testing
- Try different ad copy
- Try different images
- Adjust targeting

## ðŸ”„ PHASE 3: OPTIMIZE (Weeks 2-4)

### Test These Variables (One at a Time):

**Week 2: Test Images**
- Ad 1: Beautiful Westchester photo
- Ad 2: Newsletter screenshot
- Ad 3: Simple graphic
- Keep same text

**Week 3: Test Copy**
- Ad 1: Benefit-focused ("Never miss...")
- Ad 2: Social proof ("Join 1,000...")
- Ad 3: Specific ("30+ events...")
- Keep same image

**Week 4: Test Targeting**
- Ad Set 1: Westchester only
- Ad Set 2: + NYC weekenders
- Ad Set 3: Narrow interests (foodies, arts lovers)
- Keep same creative

### Optimization Tips:

1. **Turn off bad ads fast** (if not working after $20 spent)
2. **Scale what works** (increase budget on winning ads)
3. **Test one thing at a time** (or you won't know what worked)
4. **Give ads 2-3 days** to optimize before judging

## ðŸŽ¯ ADVANCED TARGETING (After Initial Success)

### Lookalike Audiences (After 100-200 Subs):
1. Create "Custom Audience" from your email list
2. Create "Lookalike Audience" (1%-3%)
3. Target people similar to your subscribers
4. Usually converts better than cold audience

### Retargeting (After 1,000 Page Views):
1. Create audience: "Visited landing page, didn't subscribe"
2. Show them different ad
3. Cheaper conversions (they already showed interest)

### Interest Stacking:
Narrow targeting by combining interests:
- People interested in "Local news" AND "Food and dining"
- People interested in "Arts" AND "Live in Westchester"
