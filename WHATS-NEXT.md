## ü§î KEY QUESTIONS TO ANSWER

Before you start, clarify:

1. **Do you want to be the face/voice** (like Naptown Scoop's Ryan Sneddon)?
   - Or anonymous/brand-focused (like Catskill Crew)?

2. **How much time can you commit?**
   - Weekly (4-5 hours/week): Sustainable
   - 3x/week (10-15 hours/week): Growth mode
   - Daily (20+ hours/week): Need team

3. **What's your revenue priority?**
   - Ads only (easier, lower revenue)
   - Ads + Premium (Naptown model, medium complexity)
   - Ads + Premium + Shop + Club (Catskill Crew model, highest revenue)

4. **What's your exit goal?**
   - Lifestyle business ($100-200K/year)
   - Scale & sell (build to $500K+/year, then sell)
   - Build empire (40K+ subs, $1M+/year)
