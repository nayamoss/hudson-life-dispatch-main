# What Great Local Newsletters Do

Research based on: Naptown Scoop, Catskill Crew, Salina311, and Winnipeg Digest

---

## What People Love

### 1. Food & Restaurant Coverage
**Most popular content across all newsletters**
- New restaurant openings
- Quick reviews and recommendations  
- "Where to eat this weekend" guides
- Chef/owner profiles

### 2. Comprehensive Events Calendar
**Essential service - people rely on this**
- 20-50+ events listed per newsletter
- Format: `Location | Event Name | Time | Cost`
- Free community event submission form
- Covers everything (music, arts, kids, sports, community)

### 3. Local News That Matters
- City council decisions (what happened, why it matters)
- New businesses opening/closing
- Development updates
- School news
- Quick, digestible news bites

### 4. Arts & Culture
- Gallery openings
- Theater/performance previews
- Artist profiles
- Museum exhibitions

### 5. People Profiles
- Local business owners
- Community leaders
- Artists and makers
- "Day in the life" features

## What Makes Them Successful

1. **Consistency** - They publish on schedule, always. Readers build habits around them.

2. **Hyperlocal** - Street-level detail. They cover small stories (business anniversaries, local achievements).

3. **Personality** - Friendly, conversational writing. Not boring corporate news.

4. **Service** - They help people navigate community life (what's happening, where to eat, what's closed).

5. **Community Voice** - Reader submissions, two-way conversation, featuring locals.

6. **Visual** - Photos in every newsletter, Instagram integration.

7. **Mobile-Friendly** - Most readers on phones. Clean, easy-to-read design.

## Hudson Life Dispatch Structure

### Newsletter Schedule
**Weekly** (Sunday mornings)
- Catskill Crew model: Deep, thematic content
- Sustainable starting pace
- Can scale to 3x/week later

### Weekly Newsletter Structure
**Sunday Morning Edition:**
- Lead story (biggest local news or feature)
- **People Profile** (everyday people interviews - FREE)
- Food/Restaurant spotlight
- Comprehensive week-ahead events (30-50 events)
- Arts & culture feature
- Community news/quick hits
- *Future: Business features (PAID)*

### Priority Content
1. **People Profiles** - Everyday people interviews (weekly feature)
2. **Food/Restaurants** - 1-2 stories per newsletter
3. **Events** - 30-50 events for the week ahead
4. **Local News** - Lead story
5. **Arts/Culture** - 1-2 features per newsletter

### Tools Needed
- Beehiiv ($49-99/month) - newsletter platform
- Airtable (free) - event database + submission form
- Canva ($13/month) - graphics
- Instagram (free) - community engagement
- **Total cost: ~$60-110/month**

## That's It

The formula is simple:
- **Food** (people love this)
- **Events** (essential service)
- **Local news** (keep informed)
