### If You Have <50 Subscribers:

**Something's not working. Investigate:**

Questions to ask:
1. Is the newsletter content good? (are people opening it?)
2. Are Facebook posts being seen? (check group rules, post timing)
3. Is the value proposition clear? (do people understand what they get?)
4. Are you targeting the right audience? (maybe different towns?)

**Options**:
- Pivot messaging
- Try different distribution channels (Instagram first?)
- Narrow geography (just Ossining to start?)
- Talk to 5 people who didn't subscribe: "Why not?"
