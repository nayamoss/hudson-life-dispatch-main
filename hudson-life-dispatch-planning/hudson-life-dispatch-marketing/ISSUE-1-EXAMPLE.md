## üìñ This Week's Spotlight: Maria's Bakery in Dobbs Ferry

You know that bakery on Main Street that you've driven past 100 times but never stopped at?

Go there.

Maria opened it 3 months ago. She's originally from Sicily. She makes everything herself. The cannoli are stupid good.

I stopped in last Tuesday. She was behind the counter, covered in flour, laughing with a customer about her oven breaking down the day before. She had to bake everything at 4am when it finally got fixed.

That's the thing about small businesses in our towns - they're barely holding on, but they're the reason our towns feel like *something* instead of just another strip of chain stores.

Maria's open Tue-Sun, 7am-3pm. Cash only. The sfogliatelle is my recommendation if you've never had it before.
