## Sleepy Coffee, Too

**Opened:** April 2024

**Type:** Non-profit café with a mission

**Rating:** 4.9⭐ on Google

**Address:** 110 Beekman Avenue, Sleepy Hollow, NY 10591

**Phone:** (914) 909-5644

**Website:** [sleepycoffeetoo.org](https://sleepycoffeetoo.org)

**Why It's Great:** Sleepy Coffee, Too is more than just a great coffee shop—it's a café with purpose. This non-profit establishment provides training and employment opportunities for people with disabilities, creating an inclusive environment where everyone can thrive.

**The Mission:** By visiting Sleepy Coffee, Too, you're supporting a business model that prioritizes community inclusion and provides meaningful employment opportunities. It's proof that exceptional coffee and social good can go hand in hand.

**The Experience:**
- Cozy, welcoming atmosphere that lives up to Sleepy Hollow's charm
- Variety of coffee drinks prepared with care
- Fresh pastries and light fare
- Friendly, attentive service

**What Makes It Special:**
- **Social Impact:** Every cup supports employment for people with disabilities
- **Community Focus:** A gathering place that strengthens community bonds
- **Quality Commitment:** Great coffee and service, not just a good cause
- **Inclusive Environment:** Everyone is welcome and valued

**Perfect For:**
- Socially conscious coffee lovers
- Anyone who believes business can be a force for good
- Community members looking to support local initiatives
- Visitors who want to experience Sleepy Hollow's welcoming spirit

**Community Impact:** Since opening in April 2024, Sleepy Coffee, Too has become a model for how cafés can serve both excellent coffee and the broader community. It's a place where great coffee, social good, and community connection blend perfectly.
