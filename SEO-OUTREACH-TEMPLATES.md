## Best Practices

1. **Personalize Every Email**: Change [Name], [Venue Name], and specific details for each recipient
2. **Research First**: Check their website to see if they have a partners/media page before asking
3. **Provide Value**: Always explain what they get from the partnership
4. **Be Specific**: Mention specific events you've covered or plan to cover
5. **Follow Up**: Most backlinks come after 1-2 follow-ups
6. **Be Gracious**: Thank them whether they say yes or no
7. **Track Everything**: Use the tracking template to stay organized

## Success Metrics

- **Week 1**: Send 10 emails → Target 2-3 responses
- **Week 2**: Send 10 more emails + follow up on Week 1 → Target 2-3 links secured
- **Week 3**: Send 10 more emails + follow up → Target 3-4 links total
- **Week 4**: Finalize partnerships → Target 5-7 quality backlinks from local sources

By end of January: 15-20 quality backlinks from Westchester County venues, chambers, and organizations.
